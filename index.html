February 12, 2021

Dear friend,

Ni hao! That means hello in Mandarin! I am writing this letter to you all the way from China. Today is Chinese New Year. We celebrate our new year on a different day than January 1st because our new year is based on a different calendar. Our calendar uses the moon instead of the sun. Today, I will be eating dumplings for good luck and watching "Lion Dances". Here's a picture of a lion dance below!



The lions scare away bad spirits and help us bring in good luck for the year. Even though you might not celebrate this holiday, I still hope you have a wonderful day and a lucky year!

Best,

Wu Shasha
